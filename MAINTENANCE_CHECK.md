# 🛠️ WhiteFlows Elite: Weekly Maintenance Protocol

To ensure your platform maintains its "Mastery" status, follow this simple checklist every **Monday Morning**.

---

### **1. SMTP Authenticity Check 📧**
*   **The Risk**: If your Gmail "App Password" is revoked or your account has an issue, leads will stop arriving.
*   **The Check**: Submit a "Test Lead" on the website yourself.
*   **Success**: If you receive the notification email in 10 seconds, the engine is 100% healthy.

### **2. Intelligence Digest Review 📊**
*   **The Check**: Verify you received the "Daily Intelligence Digest" email.
*   **Success**: This confirms your database is active and the background scheduler is running perfectly.

### **3. Google Search Console Pulse 📈**
*   **The Check**: Open [Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console).
*   **The Action**: 
    1. Click **"Indexing"** in the left menu. Ensure "Successful" pages are increasing and "Errors" are 0.
    2. Click **"Sitemaps"** and verify that your `sitemap.xml` status is "Success".
*   **Success**: This keeps your ranking "Green." If you see a red error, it means Google is blocking your business from search results!

### **4. Disk Space Guard 💾**
*   **The Check**: Check the Render Dashboard for "Disk Usage."
*   **Success**: We have a 1GB limit. For a typical lead business, this will last for **years**, but it's good to check once a month.

---
**Status**: 🟢 System Mastery Maintained.
